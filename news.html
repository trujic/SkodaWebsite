News

New Infotainment Generation

ŠKODA KAROQ, KODIAQ and SUPERB with new infotainment. Laura ŠKODA Digital Assistant and wireless SmartLink. Enhanced personalisation. Latest USB-C ports.

ŠKODA OCTAVIA G-TEC

Eco-friendly and cost-effective alternative: 1.5 TSI produces 96 kW (130 PS). CO2 emissions in CNG mode are around 25 per cent lower. Natural gas range of 500 km* in the WLTP cycle.

MOGUL Test-Rally Sosnová

ŠKODA Motorsport operates again at full swing. ŠKODA privateer Petr Semerád wins MOGUL Test-Rally Sosnová. Factory driver Jan Kopecký was delayed by a puncture.

24 Hours of Le Mans

70 years ago today: ŠKODA’s only start in the famous 24 Hours of Le Mans. Specially developed ŠKODA Sport had a lightweight aluminium body, based on the 1101/1102 ‘Tudor’ model.

ŠKODA 2021 Model Year

Countless new features: New steering wheels, engine line-up converted to Euro 6d emissions standard, Area View and Trailer Assist further refined.

Austrian Alpine Rally

110 years of the Austrian Alpine Rally: Laurin & Klement celebrate success in the most challenging rally of their time.

3 Millionth EA211 Engine

ŠKODA builds EA211 engines in four specifications for its own cars and other Volkswagen Group models, currently as three- and four-cylinder variants with capacities ranging from 1.0 to 1.6 l.

ŠKODA OCTAVIA: Infotainment

ŠKODA OCTAVIA’s infotainment system and vehicle settings controlled via touch screen; gesture and voice control available. Innovative operating concepts via smartphone.

ŠKODA FABIA Rally2 evo

When safety becomes more important than speed – the ŠKODA FABIA Rally2 evo’s role as guardian angel. Roughly 34 metres of steel tubes strengthen cockpit.

ŠKODA Student Car Tradition

ŠKODA Vocational School students built four prototypes of the ŠKODA BUGGY type 736 back in 1975. Spider version of the ŠKODA SCALA in 2020 will be the seventh official ŠKODA student car.

Mobility Innovation Hub

ŠKODA AUTO backs Czech national programme Mobility Innovation Hub. Programme will support the development, implementation and testing of new mobility services and technologies.

The New ŠKODA OCTAVIA SCOUT

Boasting off-road virtues, the new-generation OCTAVIA continues the SCOUT models’ success story. Multifunctional lifestyle estate with a rugged appearance and 15 mm more ground clearance.

ŠKODA Motorsport eChallenge

Online competition ŠKODA Motorsport eChallenge writes ongoing young success story; third round at Rally Catalunya (10/06/–14/06/2020) offers special challenge on tarmac.

Change to Board of Management

ŠKODA AUTO appoints Karsten Schnake as its new Board Member for Purchasing. Predecessor Dieter Seemann steps down as part of an early retirement scheme.

COVID Mobility Race

International hackathon of ŠKODA AUTO DigiLab and ŠKODA AUTO held online for the first time. 52 teams from 19 countries worked on innovative digital solutions for the car trade.

Technical Innovations from Motorsport

Four-wheel drive, turbocharging, direct fuel injection and downsized engines prove their reliability on rally tracks as well as in ŠKODA road cars during daily use.

World Bicycle Day

ŠKODA commemorates its historical roots on the occasion of World Cycling Day. Current bike collection includes a total of 17 models from children’s bicycles to e-bikes.

Ergo comfort seats

The ŠKODA OCTAVIA’s front seats are exceptionally back-friendly and feature support functions and a wide range of adjustment options, massage and ventilation functions.

ŠKODA Connect App

All the key data in a single app. Updated ŠKODA Connect smartphone app for enhanced ease of use and convenience. Wireless in-vehicle connectivity with one-time registration.

#Ride2Unite

International cycling campaign on We Love Cycling and social media. Professionals share their experiences, discussing various cycling related topics with amateur riders and fans via livestream.
